Book Synopsis

This valuable workbook and CD set can turn any English speaker into an accomplished debater.The student-centered format is suitable for large and small classes alike. Each of the ten chapters offers a "language focus" and a "debate focus." As students learn new debate skills, they also build important language skills. Make Your Point! will benefit everyone from high school students to business people. A CD for all practice conversations is attached on the inside cover.
