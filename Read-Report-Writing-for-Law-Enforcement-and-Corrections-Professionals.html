Book Synopsis

Provides a holistic approach addressing all aspects of the intricacies of the criminal justice report writing system, including practical features reinforcing essential writing principles, video scenarios that provide context for writing assignments, and examples of forms and documents most commonly used by law enforcement professionals. KEY TOPICS: Report Writing, Criminal Justice, Law Enforcement, Corrections MARKET: Students considering a career in the field of criminal justice, whether they are attending a college or university, or enrolled in a law enforcement or corrections academy.
